Hello,

my name is Noah Anderson. I was born in Tacoma, WA and raised in many places around the world including Washington, Germany, Italy, and now Colorado. I was inspired to be an actor in the 6th grade and did my first play in the 7th grade. Since then I have performed in several plays and musicals, auditioned and got into a performing arts high school as a major in Theatre, worked professionally on stage and camera, achieved the critics choice of a main stage performance at the State and National Conference levels, and have been accepted into several Theatre/Acting programs across the nation. I aspire to act and perform because not only do I believe that it can most definitely effect and change the world, but it also makes me happy. Performing entails excitement, adrenaline, experience and connection all of which I strive for in my every day life. I believe that through my craft and artistry I can make a difference.
